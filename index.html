About Angel's Crafts

Angel's started designing and crocheting blankets to fill the time while caring for family. After Covid-19 hit, Angel needed to fill her time while being "stuck" at home. This transitioned into designing themes for scarves, blankets, etc to further occupy her time.

As these treasures were given out to family members as gifts, friends and family began asking for additional designs to be made for purchase. Hence the start up and Angel's Craft Store. Over time, she has added many custom designs, upon requests.

Soon, Angel plans to add custom shirts, car window decals, milestone blankets, and tumblers to her current crochet creations.
